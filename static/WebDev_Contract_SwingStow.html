Web Development Services Agreement

This Web Development Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 11, 2026 between Jelly Development LLC ("Developer"), represented by Jarod Peachey, and SwingStow LLC ("Client"), represented by Michael McCuistion. Both parties agree to the following terms.

1. Scope of Work

Developer will design and build a professional website for Client under the Growth Plan. Deliverables include:

  • Up to 5 website pages
  • Responsive design (mobile, tablet, and desktop)
  • Performance optimization
  • Local SEO setup
  • 3 months of complimentary hosting after launch
  • Integration of Client-provided domain, logo, and branding

Client responsibilities: Client shall provide the domain name, logo files, brand guidelines, and any written content before or at the start of the project. Delays in providing these materials may extend the project timeline.

2. Project Timeline

The project is estimated to be completed within 4 weeks from the date of this Agreement and receipt of the initial payment.

Week 1
Define scope, confirm page list, and gather project requirements
Week 2
Design mockups and receive Client approval
Week 3
Copywriting and content finalization
Week 4
Development, QA testing, and site deployment
3. Payment Terms

The total project fee is $2,800.00, payable in two installments:

Deposit (50%) $1,400.00 Due upon signing
Final Payment (50%) $1,400.00 Due upon completion, prior to launch
Total $2,800.00

Invoices are due upon receipt. A late fee of 5% may be applied to any payment not received within 7 days of its due date.

4. Intellectual Property & Failed Payment Protection

All work product, source code, design files, and the website itself (the "Work") remain the sole property of Jelly Development LLC until the final payment is received in full. Client acquires no ownership rights during this period.

Upon receipt of the final payment, Developer will transfer full ownership of the Work to SwingStow LLC and deliver all source files and credentials within 5 business days.

If the final payment is not received within 14 days of the project completion notice, Developer reserves the right to suspend the website and retain all work product until payment is made. Developer will provide written notice before taking any such action.

The initial deposit of $1,400.00 is non-refundable once work has begun.

5. Hosting

Developer will provide 3 months of complimentary hosting starting on the launch date. After that period, Client may continue hosting through Developer at the then-current rate or migrate to a provider of their choice. Developer will assist with a one-time migration at no additional cost.

6. Post-Launch Content Edits

Content edits or updates requested after launch are billed at $30.00 per hour. Developer will provide an estimate before beginning any work expected to exceed 2 hours.

7. Revisions During Development

This Agreement includes up to three rounds of revisions per page during development at no additional cost. Additional revisions or scope changes will be quoted separately and require written approval.

8. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information or business data shared during this project and not to disclose it to third parties without prior written consent.

9. Warranties & Liability

Developer will complete the Work in a professional manner consistent with industry standards. Developer does not warrant uninterrupted operation of third-party platforms or hosting services. Developer's total liability shall not exceed the total amount paid under this Agreement.

10. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 14 days written notice. Client will be invoiced for all work completed to date. The deposit is non-refundable. Ownership of all work product remains with Developer until any outstanding balance is paid in full.

11. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the state in which Jelly Development LLC is registered. Both parties agree to first attempt to resolve any disputes through good-faith negotiation.

12. Entire Agreement

This Agreement represents the full understanding between both parties and supersedes any prior discussions or agreements. Any modifications must be made in writing and signed by both parties.
